About us

The NoSoloCasting portal 's main objective is to put candidates from various artistic activities in contact with companies and professionals so that they can summon them to different castings or hire them directly.

NoSoloCasting is not a recruitment agency and its mission is to act as a mediator and provide said service without any other interest or direct collaboration. NoSoloCasting is made up of a team of experts related to different audiovisual media and with a long history in professional communication.
